About Us

In keeping with this motto, Aparoksha sponsors an annual Tech Tour, XPLORE, an a fun learning experience as well as a social initiative to increase technical awareness, build enthusiasm in people and give back to the society. Currently in its first iteration, XPLORE has managed to reach over 2000 students and in various schools and college across India, and plans to spread its root even more in the years to come.

Who are We and Just What Exactly Do We Do?

The XPLORE Team consists of highly enthusiastic and experienced developers, coders and orators, who much like great Master Yoda believe that one must “pass on what you have learned”. We organise Quick Talks on popular issues in contemporary technology and hold Discussions on how to make it big in various competitions like ACM-ICPC and GSoC, explained by veterans in the field themselves.

Through X-PLORE, we also indulge you a with glimpse of Aparoksha but in your very own city through various competitions and give you a real-feel of the adrenaline and hype of one of the biggest Tech Fests of North India.

We organise enticing quizzes and mini Hackathons to compel you to think out of the box, rack your brains and solving mind-boggling questions, all while standing a chance to win Aparoksha goodies, exclusive fest passes ,stickers and more! So go forth, participate and stand a chance to win amazing prizes! Grab the opportunity as it knocks your door, chisel your brains and gear up for the challenges to come. Find your city here!
